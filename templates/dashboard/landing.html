How It Works

Get from customer data to print-ready card in under two minutes.

1

Sign Up & Top Up Your Wallet

Create your operator account in seconds and load coins via secure UPI payment QR. No monthly fees — pay only for what you generate.

2

Enter Customer Details

Fill in the form fields — name, photo, land info, family members, or scheme details. The live preview updates in real time as you type.

3

Choose a Design & Save

Pick from multiple premium card designs. Each card is saved individually with its own design — creating a new card never overwrites old ones.

4

Add to Print Queue & Print

Queue multiple cards together, then print them all at once on A4 with accurate dashed cut guides. Perfectly scaled — no layout distortion.
